A glimpse into what Paris has to offer...

Montmartre

An artist's village, Montmartre is a unique village on an extremely famous hill right in Paris. With numerous street artists and the beautiful Basicila of Sacre-Coeur, opened in 1914, this location is a must for tourists. It also has the best view of all of Paris.

Musée du Louvre

Hosting the famous painting of the Mona Lisa, the Louvre is one of the most prestigious and largest art museums in the world, conating over 380,000 objects and 35,000 works of art. The architecture alone is one worth seeing from the outside with its two iconic glass pyramids.

Arc de Triomphe

One of the most famous monuments in Paris right at the end of Champs-Elysees. The arch stands as a powerful, unified symbol for France. All the names of the generals and wars fought can be found engraved on the inside. The rooftop gives one of the best views of Paris right from the center of the city.
