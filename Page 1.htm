KEEP IN MIND: To travel abroad, Babson requires you to submit the application a semester prior to your preferred departure date. For example, if you want to travel in the Spring then you must submit the application in the Fall, and if you want to travel in the Fall then you must submit the application in the Spring the semester prior.
