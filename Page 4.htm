Paris Travel Guide
Trips from Paris

Europe is great for traveling. Booking cheap flights and train tickets is extremely possible. There are many great cities and places one can easily visit when in Paris, the list can go on forever. I had the chance of traveling to three different cities during my time abroad, Prague, Amsterdam, and Milan. And will share my travel guides for these cities with you.

Prague

Where to Stay

So there are three good places to stay in Prague and they all surround Old Town. Old town is where everything nightlife related really goes down in the city and probably where you will spend most of your time since you’re there for just a weekend. You ideally want to be located close to a subway stop.

How to Get Around

I really recommend using public transport in Prague. The metro and tram system are both extremely easy to use and can get you anywhere you need in Prague. I recommend buying a 3 day pass for about $10. You buy them at Metro stations or at some major tram locations. Below is a map of the metro system in Prague. You’ll probably be staying around the stops circled in blue.However, Prague is not extremely big and you can walk places or Uber if you want. UberPop is what it’s called through the app and is cheaper than everywhere else in Europe.

Where to Eat

The food in Prague is amazing if you go to the right places. Very fair prices just about everywhere you go. You also need to know that Czech cuisine is mostly meat and potatoes, but some of the best meat and potatoes you will ever have. You don’t have to eat Czech food unless you want it, but some of the better dishes are pork knuckle, schnitzel, svickova, among others.
