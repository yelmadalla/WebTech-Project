Trips from Paris
A brief look at the beauty of Paris:

Places to visit:

Musée d’Orsay Musée de l’Orangerie Les Catacombes Eiffel Tower (ofc) Louvre Champs Elysées Arc de Triomphe Notre Dame Musee YSL Fondation Louis Vuitton

To eat:

Le relais de l’entrecôte —> only has entrecôte and fries in their menu fixed cost of 26€ and you get two portions Il était un square —> known for its burgers (you have to reserve)

To go out:

Bastille/Rue de Lappe (street with a mix of bars and clubs) Treplay Niki Bar Balajo La Machine de Moulin Rouge Concrete Wanderlust (Art school that transforms into a two level club) Rex Club (this is a cinema with an underground bar) VIP Room Silencio 38 Riv (Underground jazz) Le Baron Paris L’Arc Paris Whisper Club Paris

Bars:

Little red door (nominated one of the best bars in the world) Le Sully Rue Oberkampf (anywhere on this street) Rue mouffetard/place de la contrescarpe (anywhere on this street) Rue de la huchette Le Cavern Club (live music) Bit more fancy bars: Le duc des Lombards Le grand bleu Les chandles Gravity bar Experimental cocktail bar

Speakeasies:

Mabel Le ballroom du beefclub Le syndicat Gentlemen 1919 Hammam Bar Le lavomatic Le mezcaleria No entry Secret 8 by bar buddha
