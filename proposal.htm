Project Proposal

  • What is your website about?

    • My website will be about studying abroad and doing an exchange semester at HEC Paris. Not only will it discuss the overview and background of the studying abroad process, but also the experience at HEC as a whole alongside a travel guide of living in Paris and places to visit on a budget while abroad.


  • What is the objective of your website?

    • I derived this idea for a website as I was recently abroad at HEC Paris last semester, in the Spring 2019, and whilst being back on campus I've had many students ask me about my experience alongside other prospective students who are considering studying abroad and are considering the Exchange program I did. The objective of this website would be to allow prospective Babson students (First years to Juniors) to understand what this experience entails and to hopefully aid them in making the right decision for them in studying abroad.


  • Who is your target audience?

    • The target audience for this website would definitely be tailored to Babson students who are interested in learning more about this experience and that are potentially considering HEC Paris as one of their options. More specifically, it would be Sophomores and Juniors who are currently in the process of choosing where to study abroad or are already applying for the next semester.


  • What content do you plan to include in each page of your website?

    • For the pages of my website, I'd like to include one page with the background of studying abroad at Babson, what it entails and how the initial process actually is with the International Student Office, discussing more specifically HEC Paris and why it should be an option.
    The next page would be about HEC Paris and how it works, alongside tips and tricks for getting by, where to live, how classes are, comparisons to Babson College, their offerings and value it can provide.
    The third page would be a travel guide for Paris, as HEC Paris is out of the city by about 40 minutes to an hour, going to Paris isn't as simple and it's very dispersive. I want to have a nice travel guide with options of where to eat, what to go see, and how to make the best of your time in Paris and its surroundings.
    The fourth page would be a travel guide for the rest of Europe, cities that I recommend to go see during the time abroad other than in France, with some brief travel guides for those cities too.
